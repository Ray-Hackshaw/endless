ENDLESS

Endless is a catalogue of work and backlog of previous projects, as well as a hub for resources I find useful and/or interesting. I wanted a platform to better explain my ideas and reasoning for personal projects, more than just what lives on Github. I wanted to have everything as a stream of ideas that were all in one place. It is updated periodically with any new projects as well as changes to old work I may have revisited. Currently this exists through a subdomain of my website, however I am looking to migrate this elsewhere soon.

Disclaimer: this site is being worked on continually - some areas may be broken or missing from time to time.
